Hi, I'm Pawel, the creator of this tool. It is a part of a larger project aimed at facilitating the analysis of complex structures. Organisational structures are called bipartite directed networks in the network science nomenclature. There is a number of techniques and methodologies developed by the researchers that can be used to analyse this type of networks. Practitioners can use them to establish the key players or entities with similar structures. Statistical similarities between already studied structures can be used to determine entities that might require additional due diligence. My contact email is aspiela@gmail.com.
